Portofino Hike

Portofino Hike

Portofino is an Italian famous destination for many celebrities and tourists. It's promontory is a Regional Park that offerts eighty kilometers of hiking trails with many cultural and historical sites hidden around the mountains.


Tourists visit the Park all year around; there are between 100,000 and 130,000 hikers yearly. Nonetheless, many are the accidents on the trails, due to the wrong choice of hiking gear, difficulty of the hiking trails and the geological structure of the mountain.

Portofino Hike is an app that simplifies hiking. Portofino Hike features an interactive map in which the users will be able to locate themselves while hiking, minimizing the possibilities for the hikers to get lost or injured. Furthermore, it will highlight the points of interest.



CHOOSING THE RIGHT TRAIL


Lists of trails are divided intuitively for hikers to have a detailed preview and understanding of the path, indication signs and points of interest.


The side menu lets the user access quickly to the main features of the app
